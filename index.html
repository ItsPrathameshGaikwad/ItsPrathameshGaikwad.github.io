Financial Data Analysis Project

Project Overview

This project analyzes a financial sales dataset to understand product performance, country profitability, and customer segment contribution. The analysis was performed using Power BI by cleaning, transforming, and visualizing the data to generate business insights.

Dataset Fields Used

  • Country
  • Date
  • Product
  • Segment
  • Gross Sales
  • Profit
  • Profit Margin
  • Sale Price
  • Discounts
  • COGS
  • Units Sold

Data Cleaning Process

  • Checked for missing or inconsistent values in sales and profit columns.
  • Formatted the Date column to enable monthly trend analysis.
  • Calculated Profit Margin using profit and sales values.
  • Verified numerical columns such as Gross Sales, Units Sold, and Discounts.
  • Standardized categorical fields like Product, Segment, and Country.

Data Visualization

The Power BI dashboard includes the following visualizations:

  • Total Profit KPI
  • Total Sales KPI
  • Total Units Sold KPI
  • Profit Margin KPI
  • Bar Chart showing Total Profit per Country
  • Bar Chart showing Total Sales per Product
  • Donut Chart showing Units Sold per Segment
  • Line Chart showing Monthly Sales Trend by Country

Business Insights

  • France generated the highest profit among all countries.
  • Paseo is the highest selling product.
  • The Government segment contributes the largest share of unit sales.
  • Sales show strong growth during the final months of the year.
  • Profit margin across the dataset is approximately 13.21%.
